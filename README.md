# Diwali-sales-analysis
Diwali Sales Analysis | Customer Behavior & Revenue Insights using Python
This project analyzes Diwali sales transaction data to evaluate customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and overall sales trends using Python.
The objective is to extract meaningful business insights from raw retail data and identify buying patterns, high-performing categories, and demographic influences on sales.
 Key KPIs Analyzed

Total Sales Transactions

1.Month-To-Date (MTD) Sales

2.Total Revenue Generated

3.Average Order Value (AOV)

4.Customer Demographics (Age, Gender, Occupation)

5.Product Category PerformanceAnalysis Performed

6.Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

7.Regional (State-wise) Sales Distribution

8.Gender & Age Group Purchase Behavior

9.Occupation-wise Spending Analysi

10.Product Category & Sub-category Breakdown

11.Impact of Marital Status on Spending

# Business Insights

Identified top-performing product categories and regions contributing maximum revenue
Analyzed customer demographics to understand target segments for marketing campaigns
Evaluated spending behavior across occupations and age groups
Assessed seasonal buying patterns during Diwali to optimize inventory and promotions
